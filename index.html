About Me

I'm a gameplay programmer with a BSc in Game Software Engineering from Bournemouth University, currently expanding my skillset through full-stack web development at Founders & Coders.

My strength lies in building modular game systems - whether it's implementing Unreal's Gameplay Ability System from scratch, designing enemy AI with custom state machines, or developing PBR shaders in OpenGL.

I've shipped a TIGER Award-nominated game with a team of 11, and I'm currently developing Dungeon Slayer, a wave-based action RPG built entirely in C++ and UE5.

Living across Syria, Italy, and the UK has taught me to adapt quickly and approach problems from multiple perspectives - skills that translate directly to debugging complex game systems.
